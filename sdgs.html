Global Impact

SDGs & Competition Themes

Sparkie Buddy aligns with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals and the Codeavour 7.0 themes — driving real-world impact beyond the competition floor.

UN Sustainable Development Goals

SDGs We Address

Our project directly contributes to three of the United Nations' 17 Sustainable Development Goals through its features and mission.

SDG 3

Good Health & Well-being

Promoting mental health support and safe, healthy lives for children with ADHD through technology-assisted monitoring, structured routines, and AI-guided care.

SDG 10

Reduced Inequalities

Making specialized ADHD support accessible to all children regardless of background or location — bridging the gap in neurodiversity support worldwide.

SDG 12

Responsible Consumption

Encouraging mindful, healthy nutrition habits through our AI-powered diet planning feature, promoting responsible eating for children with ADHD.

Codeavour 7.0 Themes

Competition Themes Covered

Our project addresses both major themes of the Codeavour 7.0 International Competition.

💡

Tech for Wellness, Equity for All

Leveraging robotics and AI to bring equitable wellness solutions to children with ADHD. We believe technology should empower every child, regardless of their neurological differences.

🌿

Sustainable Eating, Healthy Living

Using machine learning to guide healthy, sustainable dietary habits for children with ADHD — improving their overall well-being, focus, and daily performance through nutrition.
