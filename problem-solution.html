Challenge & Response

The Problem. Our Solution.

Millions of children with ADHD lack dedicated support tools. Sparkie Buddy was born to change that — one child at a time.

The Challenge

Why This Matters

When we began researching, we found a striking gap. No robots or dedicated projects existed to support children living with ADHD on a day-to-day basis.

⚠️

The Gap We Discovered

The main problem we faced when starting this project was that there were no existing projects or robots made to help people with Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD). So, we began creating this project to give people with ADHD tools to feel better mentally, focus more, and complete their daily tasks easily.

Our robot and software are specially made to help people with ADHD — especially kids — handle their symptoms and live happy, independent lives. We hope to make a good impact on society through this effort.

Rising ADHD diagnoses globally No dedicated robot support tools Impacts attention, focus & emotion
Our Response

How Sparkie Buddy Helps

To tackle the challenges we've seen, we've come up with Sparkie Buddy — a robot that's all about being there for kids with ADHD.

🎯Gentle Task Nudges

The main idea is to gently nudge kids to get their daily tasks done — without overwhelming them. Sparkie Buddy makes routines feel friendly and achievable.

🧘Research-Backed Exercises

We've put together special exercises based on solid research from Wikipedia, Google, and Firefox. They help calm children's minds and make them more focused and attentive.

📡Real-Time Monitoring

Sparkie Buddy keeps an eye on how kids are doing throughout the day and alerts caregivers when support is needed, ensuring children are always safe.

🧠✨

Smart. Caring. Always There.

Sparkie Buddy is more than a robot — it's a companion. Designed with compassion and powered by AI to support every child's unique journey.
