About the Project

What is Sparkie Buddy?

A smart system designed to help kids with ADHD thrive — by monitoring their progress, guiding their tasks, and keeping them safe.

Our Story

Built for Every Child

Sparkie Buddy is a smart system that helps keep an eye on how kids with ADHD are doing and makes sure they're safe. ADHD is a condition that affects how the brain manages attention, motivation, emotions, and impulses.


A study in Finland showed that more and more kids are being diagnosed with ADHD. Even though it can be tough for kids with ADHD to handle everyday things, there haven't been many complete solutions or programs to help them live better, learn more, and grow as individuals. Sparkie Buddy changes that.

Understanding ADHD

What Does ADHD Affect?

🧠

Attention

Children with ADHD often struggle to maintain focus on tasks, leading to incomplete work and frustration in school and at home.

💪

Motivation

Starting tasks or sustaining effort — especially for activities that aren't immediately rewarding — can be extremely difficult.

😤

Emotions

Emotional regulation is harder for kids with ADHD. They may experience intense feelings and struggle to manage frustration or excitement.

⚡

Impulses

Acting before thinking, interrupting others, or making hasty decisions are common challenges that affect social and academic life.

"Every child deserves tools that help them focus, grow, and thrive — no matter how their brain works."

Sparkie Buddy was built on this belief. Our team is committed to making ADHD support accessible, compassionate, and powered by the latest technology.
