Key Features of Sparkie Buddy

Packed with cutting-edge technology — all designed with one goal: helping every child succeed on their own terms.

What We Built

Smart Technology, Human Heart

Sparkie Buddy brings together IoT, AI, machine learning, and voice control into one friendly robot system that works seamlessly in a child's daily life.

📡

IoT Remote Control

Internet of Things support lets parents and caregivers check and control Sparkie Buddy from anywhere using mobile or web apps — keeping families connected wherever they are.

IoT
🤖

AI Chatbot

An intelligent AI chatbot built into Sparkie Buddy offers conversation, encouragement, and guided support whenever a child needs it — like a calm, always-available friend.

Artificial Intelligence
🎙️

Voice & Gesture Control

Kids can interact naturally through voice commands or hand gestures — no complicated screens needed. This makes Sparkie Buddy truly accessible for all children.

Accessibility
🔔

Real-Time Feedback

Instant warnings and notifications keep caregivers and the child informed throughout the day. Help is always available at exactly the right moment when it's needed most.

Live Monitoring
📈

Machine Learning

Sparkie Buddy learns from a child's past tasks and behavioral patterns, continuously improving its suggestions and support — growing smarter as the child grows.

Machine Learning
🥗

AI Diet Planning

A personalized diet planning feature powered by ML helps ensure kids with ADHD get the right nutrition for better focus, energy, and overall daily well-being.

Nutrition AI
