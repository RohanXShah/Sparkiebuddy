Our Members

The Sparkie Buddy Team

United by one mission: making sure every child with ADHD has the support they deserve.

Alex is a passionate coder and robotics enthusiast who led the hardware integration of Sparkie Buddy. He has won multiple regional science competitions and dreams of building technology for social good.

AI & ML Engineer

Priya Sharma

Priya designed the AI chatbot and machine learning algorithms powering Sparkie Buddy's adaptive features. She is deeply interested in using AI to bridge healthcare gaps for underserved communities.

UX & Research

Mentor

Our Mission

We are students united by one belief — that every child with ADHD deserves tools that help them thrive. Through Sparkie Buddy, we hope to reach thousands of families and make a lasting impact on how the world supports neurodiverse children.
